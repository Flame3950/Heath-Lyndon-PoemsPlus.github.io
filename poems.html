A Healthy Family

What once was, still now is,
A family filled of love, a family filled of kids,
Love not pretending, a love never ending,
A love that people should be recommending,
Open communication, Affirmation and Trust,
A sense of tradition and shared leisure time is only just,
A sense of right and wrong and responsibility,
A shared religious vision and no hostility,
A healthy family is within everyone's ability,
Attained with growth and open possibilities,

Soaring to new heights through Generosity

Soaring to new heights through generosity,
Spreading love and help through the community,
Giving assistance where need be,
Trying to channel the true you and me,
Contributing positively to all who are suffering from poverty,
Lending time, make friends, and being free to see what generosity can be,
Use generosity to help unite the society,
Everyone should remember you for your many generous qualities,

Uniqueness

Uniqueness, be unique to yourself,
Your unique to God and everyone else,
From your hair, to your eyes, and your skin as a matter of fact,
You are made different and it's just as simple as that,
Your uniqueness is greatness, that must be appreciated,
Each given quality must be equally demonstrated,
Let no one discourage your uniqueness,
Just be proud to be yourself,
Because that's the way God made you,
And God is the best,
